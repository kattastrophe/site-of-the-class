About

Lilith is a short comic I made in my sequential imagery class spring 2023. The story follows the main character, Lilith, as she runs her flower shop. Just like the Lilith of the Valley, Lilith is the epitome of beauty, feminity, and purity. But as beautiful as she may appear, she has a dark side.
