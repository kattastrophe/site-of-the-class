About

The story of Where The Missing Socks Go follows a girl named Ashe Doe as she falls asleep at her local laundromat and is suddenly awakened by the shrill ringing of a phone. Everything following that is a string of peculiar events that suddenly lead Ashe on an adventure to another world.
